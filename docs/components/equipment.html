Tension Effects

HIGH (> 26 kg)
  • + More Control
  • + Better Feel
  • - Less Power
  • - Smaller Sweet Spot
MEDIUM (23-26 kg)

Balanced performance

LOW (< 23 kg)
  • + More Power
  • + Larger Sweet Spot
  • - Less Control
  • - Less Feel

Tip: Try starting with lower tension for more power
