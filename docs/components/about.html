Learn Tennis Basics

Welcome to Tennis for Dummies, your fun guide to understanding and enjoying the game of tennis. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to brush up on the fundamentals, we've got you covered!

Tennis is a racket sport played on a rectangular court. Players use a stringed racket to hit a ball over a net into the opponent's court. The game can be played as singles (one player per side) or doubles (two players per side).
