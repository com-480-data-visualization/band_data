Vocabulary

Key Tennis Vocabulary Explained

Ace

A serve that the opponent cannot touch with their racket.

Winner

A shot that the opponent cannot reach before it bounces twice.

Unforced Error

Missing a shot when not under pressure from opponent.

First Serve %

Percentage of first serves that land in the service box.

Break Points

Opportunities to win opponent's service game.

Rally Length

Number of shots in a point including serve.
