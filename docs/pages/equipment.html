Strings & Tension Guide

String Materials

Synthetic Gut

Most popular, good all-around performance

$10-20 | ⏱️ 10-20 hrs play

Polyester

Durable, control, less power

$15-30 | ⏱️ 20-40 hrs play

Natural Gut (no longer used)

Best feel and power, expensive

$40-80 | ⏱️ 5-15 hrs play

Hybrid

Mix of materials for custom feel

$20-50 | ⏱️ Varies

Tension Effects

HIGH (> 26 kg)
  • + More Control
  • + Better Feel
  • - Less Power
  • - Smaller Sweet Spot
MEDIUM (23-26 kg)

Balanced performance

LOW (< 23 kg)
  • + More Power
  • + Larger Sweet Spot
  • - Less Control
  • - Less Feel

Tip: Try starting with lower tension for more power

Footwear & Apparel

Court-Specific Shoes

Hard Court Shoes

👟
  • • Durable outsole
  • • Modified herringbone pattern on sole
  • • Extra toe protection
  • • Good cushioning

Clay Court Shoes

👟
  • • Full herringbone pattern on sole
  • • Prevents clay buildup
  • • Lateral support
  • • Tight upper knit

Grass Court Shoes

👟
  • • Pimpled/nub outsole
  • • Better grass traction
  • • Protects court surface
  • • Often required at clubs
