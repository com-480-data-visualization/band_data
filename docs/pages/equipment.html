Strings & Tension Guide

String Materials

Synthetic Gut

Most popular, good all-around performance

💰 $10-20 | ⏱️ 10-20 hrs play

Polyester

Durable, control, less power

💰 $15-30 | ⏱️ 20-40 hrs play

Natural Gut

Best feel and power, expensive

💰 $40-80 | ⏱️ 5-15 hrs play

Hybrid

Mix of materials for custom feel

💰 $20-50 | ⏱️ Varies

Tension Effects

HIGH (58-68 lbs)
  • + More Control
  • + Better Feel
  • - Less Power
  • - Smaller Sweet Spot
MEDIUM (52-57 lbs)

Balanced performance

LOW (45-51 lbs)
  • + More Power
  • + Larger Sweet Spot
  • - Less Control
  • - Less Feel

🎯 Beginners: Start with lower tension for more power!

Court-Specific Shoes

Hard Court Shoes

👟
  • • Durable outsole
  • • Modified herringbone pattern
  • • Extra toe protection
  • • Good cushioning
$$ $60-150

Clay Court Shoes

👟
  • • Full herringbone pattern
  • • Prevents clay buildup
  • • Lateral support
  • • Tight upper knit
$$ $60-140

Grass Court Shoes

👟
  • • Pimpled/nub outsole
  • • Better grass traction
  • • Protects court surface
  • • Often required at clubs
$$$ $80-180

Equipment Care Tips 🛠️

🎾

Store rackets at room temperature - extreme heat/cold damages frames!

🧵

Restring every 3-6 months or after 40-50 hours of play.

👟

Rotate between 2 pairs of shoes to extend their lifespan.

🧽

Clean grip regularly with damp cloth to maintain tackiness.
