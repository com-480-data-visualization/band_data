Tournament Terms to Know

Seed: Top-ranked players who are separated in the draw
Wildcard: Free entry given by tournament organizers
Qualifier: Players who earn entry through qualifying rounds
Lucky Loser: Highest-ranked player who lost in qualifying but gets in due to withdrawal
Walkover: Win awarded when opponent withdraws before match
Retirement: When a player quits during a match due to injury
