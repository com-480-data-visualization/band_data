Understanding the Tennis Court

Hover over different sections of the court below to learn about each area and its purpose in the game.

Court Dimensions

  • Singles: 78 ft × 27 ft
  • Doubles: 78 ft × 36 ft
  • Net Height: 3 ft at center
  • Service Box: 21 ft × 13.5 ft

Court Surfaces

  • Hard Court: Most common, medium speed
  • Clay Court: Slow, high bounce
  • Grass Court: Fast, low bounce
  • Carpet: Indoor, various speeds

Key Rules

  • • Ball must land inside lines
  • • Serves must go diagonally
  • • Two serve attempts allowed
  • • Ball can only bounce once
