Special Situations

Deuce (40-40)

When both players reach 40 points, it's called "Deuce". Now someone must win by 2 points!

The Deuce Cycle:
Deuce ‚Üí Player A wins point ‚Üí Ad-In (server)
Deuce ‚Üí Player B wins point ‚Üí Ad-Out (receiver)

Tiebreak (6-6)

When a set reaches 6-6, play a tiebreak to 7 points (win by 2).

Tiebreak Rules:
  • ‚Ä¢ First to 7 points (must win by 2)
  • ‚Ä¢ Serve alternates every 2 points
  • ‚Ä¢ Switch sides every 6 points
  • ‚Ä¢ Winner takes the set 7-6
